SRC Portfolios

SRC President

Mokoti Katlego

The Chairperson acts as the Spokesperson of the SRC. The Chairperson ensures that the SRC fulfills their mandate, and they are also responsible for coordinating the SRC's relationship with its various stakeholders. It is the Chairperson's role to lead with respect, to support the SRC and to ensure that the needs of students are prioritized.

The role of the Student Representative Council (SRC) is promoting and advocating the interests and welfare of students.

Vice President

Tinyiko Baloyi

src.dp@my.richfield.ac.za

081 002 3355

The Vice-Chairperson shares the Chairperson's role in overseeing the SRC. They manage the internal relations and discipline of the SRC, and they are also responsible for coordinating the SRC's Institutional Committees. The Vice-Chairperson facilitates the SRC's teambuilding and their Leadership Development.

The role of the Vice-Chairperson is participating in institutional decision-making structures. Advising and supporting the delivery of effective and efficient student support services. Managing and administering student representation at different levels.

Secretary-General

Ntombi Khumalo

src.sg@my.richfield.ac.za

081 002 5055

The Secretary-General oversees the administrative functions of the SRC, ensuring that records are accurately kept and communication is effectively managed. They support the Chairperson and Vice-Chairperson in their duties and coordinate the SRC's internal and external communications.

The Secretary-General also organizes meetings, prepares agendas, and maintains minutes of SRC meetings to ensure transparency and accountability within the SRC operations.

Treasurer

Amogelang Chaane

src.treasure@my.richfield.ac.za

081 002 1055

The Treasurer manages the financial affairs of the SRC, overseeing budgeting, expenditure, and financial planning. They ensure that all financial transactions are recorded accurately and that the SRC's funds are used responsibly and transparently.

The Treasurer works closely with other portfolio heads to ensure that financial resources are allocated effectively to support SRC initiatives and student activities.
